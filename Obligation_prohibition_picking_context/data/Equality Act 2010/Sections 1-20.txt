IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR a person (A)
TO discriminate against another (B) by treating B, because of a protected characteristic, less favourably than A treats or would treat others
UNLESS the protected characteristic is age and A can show A's treatment of B to be a proportionate means of achieving a legitimate aim
UNLESS the protected characteristic is disability, B is not a disabled person, and A treats or would treat disabled persons more favourably than A treats B.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate against another (B) by treating B, because of a combination of two relevant protected characteristics, less favourably than A treats or would treat a person who does not share either of those characteristics
UNLESS in reliance on another provision of this Act or any other enactment, A shows that A's treatment of B is not direct discrimination because of either or both of the characteristics in the combination or the combination of characteristics includes disability in circumstances where, if a claim of direct discrimination because of disability were to be brought, it would come within section 116 (special educational needs)
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a person B
TO not show that A's treatment of B is direct discrimination because of each of the characteristics in the combination (taken separately).
WHEN/IF/WHERE establishing a contravention of this Act by virtue of subsection (1)
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR A Minister of the Crown
TO by order amend this section so as to—
(a) make further provision about circumstances in which B can, or in which B cannot, establish a contravention of this Act by virtue of subsection (1);
(b) specify other circumstances in which subsection (1) does not apply.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate a disabled person (B) by treating B unfavourably because of something arising in consequence of B's disability
UNLESS A can show that the treatment is a proportionate means of achieving a legitimate aim
UNLESS A shows that A did not know, and could not reasonably have been expected to know, that B had the disability.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate a transsexual person (B) by treating B, in relation to an absence of B's that is because of gender reassignment, less favourably than A would treat B if—
(a) B's absence was because of sickness or injury, or
(b) B's absence was for some other reason and it is not reasonable for B to be treated less favourably.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate a woman by treating her unfavourably  because of a pregnancy of hers.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate a woman by treating her, in the period of 26 weeks beginning with the day on which she gives birth, unfavourably because she has given birth.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate a woman by treating her, in or after the protected period in relation to a pregnancy of hers, unfavourably —
(a) because of the pregnancy, or
(b) because of illness suffered by her in that protected period as a result of the pregnancy.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate a woman by treating her unfavourably because she is on compulsory maternity leave or on equivalent compulsory maternity leave.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate a woman by treating her unfavourably because she is exercising or seeking to exercise, or has exercised or sought to exercise, the right to ordinary or additional maternity leave or a right to equivalent maternity leave.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate against another (B) by applying a provision, criterion or practice which is discriminatory in relation to a relevant protected characteristic of B's.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR A person (A)
TO discriminate against another (B) by 
(a) applying to B a provision, criterion or practice,
(b) also applying, or intending to apply, the provision, criterion or practice to —
(i) persons who share a relevant protected characteristic, and
(ii) persons who do not share that relevant protected characteristic,
WHEN/IF/WHERE (c) B does not share that relevant protected characteristic,
(d) the provision, criterion or practice puts, or would put, persons with the relevant protected characteristic at a particular disadvantage when compared with persons who do not share the relevant protected characteristic,
(e) the provision, criterion or practice puts, or would put, B at substantively the same disadvantage as persons who do share the relevant protected characteristic
UNLESS A can show that the provision, criterion or practice is a proportionate means of achieving a legitimate aim.
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR A person A
TO take such steps as it is reasonable to have to take to avoid substantial disadvantages for disabled persons
WHEN/IF/WHERE a provision, criterion or practice of A's puts a disabled person at a substantial disadvantage in relation to a relevant matter in comparison with persons who are not disabled. 
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR A person A
TO take such steps as it is reasonable to have to take to avoid substantial disadvantages for disabled persons
WHEN/IF/WHERE a physical feature puts a disabled person at a substantial disadvantage in relation to a relevant matter in comparison with persons who are not disabled.
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR a person A
TO take such steps as it is reasonable to have to take to provide an auxiliary aid for disabled persons
WHEN/IF/WHERE a disabled person would, but for the provision of an auxiliary aid, be put at a substantial disadvantage in relation to a relevant matter in comparison with persons who are not disabled
------------------------

TO require a disabled person, in relation to whom A is required to comply with the duty, to pay to any extent A's costs of complying with the duty
UNLESS an express provision to the contrary applies
