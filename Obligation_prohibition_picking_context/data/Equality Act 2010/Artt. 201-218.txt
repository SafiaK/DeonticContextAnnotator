------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR a Minister of the Crown
TO make an order or regulations under this Act
UNLESS there is express provision to the contrary.
------------------------
IT IS OBLIGATORY
FOR Orders, regulations or rules under this Act
TO be made by statutory instrument.
UNLESS they apply to—
(a) a transitional exemption order under Part 1 of Schedule 11,
(b) a transitional exemption order under Part 1 of Schedule 12, or
(c) an order under paragraph 1(3) of Schedule 14 that does not modify an enactment.
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR Orders or regulations under this Act—
TO (a) make different provision for different purposes;
(b) include consequential, incidental, supplementary, transitional, transitory or saving provision.
------------------------
IT IS PERMITTED
FOR provision by virtue of subsection (4)(b)
TO be included in a separate order from the order that provides for the commencement to which the provision relates.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR the order or regulations contained in a statutory instrument
TO be made
WHEN/IF/WHERE the statutory instrument is subject to the affirmative procedure
UNLESS a draft of the instrument is laid before and approved by a resolution of each House of Parliament
WHEN/IF/WHERE the power to make an order or regulations under this Act is exercisable by a Minister of the Crown or the Treasury.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR the order or regulations contained in a statutory instrument
TO be made
WHEN/IF/WHERE the statutory instrument is subject to the affirmative procedure
UNLESS a draft of the instrument is laid before and approved by a resolution of the National Assembly for Wales
WHEN/IF/WHERE the power to make an order or regulations under this Act is exercisable by the Welsh Ministers.
------------------------
IT IS PROHIBITED
FOR the order or regulations contained in a statutory instrument
TO be made
WHEN/IF/WHERE the statutory instrument is subject to the affirmative procedure
UNLESS a draft of the instrument is laid before and approved by a resolution of the Scottish Parliament.
WHEN/IF/WHERE the power to make an order, regulations or rules under this Act is exercisable by the Scottish Ministers.
