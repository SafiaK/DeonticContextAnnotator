185 Annual report on exemption orders (1) After the end of each calendar year the Secretary of State must prepare a report on— (a) the exercise in that year of the power to make orders under section 183(1); (b) . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . (2) A report under subsection (1) must (in particular) contain— (a) details of each order made under section 183(1) in the year in question; (b) details of consultation carried out under section 183(4) in connection with orders made in that year under section 183(1). (3) The Secretary of State must lay before Parliament each report prepared under this section.
