105 Effect of diversionary caution (1) Where a diversionary caution is given, criminal proceedings may be instituted against the offender for the offence in respect of which the caution was given if, but only if, the offender fails without reasonable excuse to comply with any of the conditions attached to the caution. (2) The document mentioned in section 99 (2)(e) is admissible in such proceedings. (3) Where such proceedings are instituted, the diversionary caution ceases to have effect.
