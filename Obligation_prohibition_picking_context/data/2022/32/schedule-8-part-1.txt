(2) For the purposes of this paragraph— (a) the reference in section 20(3) to a provision, criterion or practice is a reference to a provision, criterion or practice applied by or on behalf of A; (b) the reference in section 20(4) to a physical feature is a reference to a physical feature of premises occupied by A; (c) the reference in section 20(3), (4) or (5) to a disabled person is to an interested disabled person.
