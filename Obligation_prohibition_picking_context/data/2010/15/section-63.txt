63 Communications (1) In their application to communications the following provisions apply in relation to a disabled person who is a pension credit member of an occupational pension scheme as they apply in relation to a disabled person who is a deferred member or pensioner member of the scheme— (a) section 61; (b) section 120; (c) section 126; (d) paragraph 19 of Schedule 8 (and such other provisions of that Schedule as apply for the purposes of that paragraph). (2) Communications include— (a) the provision of information; (b) the operation of a dispute resolution procedure.
