Part 1 Introductory 1 In this Schedule— “ the Tribunal ” means— (a) in relation to a school in England, the First-tier Tribunal; (b) in relation to a school in Wales, the Education Tribunal for Wales ; (c) in relation to a school in Scotland, the First-tier Tribunal for Scotland ; “ the English Tribunal ” means the First-tier Tribunal; “ the Welsh Tribunal ” means the Education Tribunal for Wales ; “ the Scottish Tribunal ” means the First-tier Tribunal for Scotland ; “ responsible body ” is to be construed in accordance with section 85.
